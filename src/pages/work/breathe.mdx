# BREATHE

## Be present.

In these busy times, we can all use a bit of relaxation. BREATHE aims to provide a quick, low-barrier way to get going with [breathing exercises](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pranayama).

Count down to bliss with this gentle, minimalistic web app.

The app offers both complete beginners or aspiring yogis with a simple way to start their journey.

By offering presets as a helpful starting point and providing simple slider controls, you can customize your practice. The experience can be further enhanced by choosing your own background color or picking a different scene to match your mood.
